A simple roguelike written in Java on libGDX

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

In 2024 you work as a scientist at a super secret facility called \#@"*$
You and the other scientists are working on a thing called the DIME Gate.

01 January 2025
When the day comes to the activation, you prepare and then.....
Everything goes black.
You wake up at home and wonder that this was just a dream, then you notice
a letter on your desk: "We had to evacuate the facility. Now you're all safe."

30 years pass...
Meteorology stations detect strange weather anomalies in Death Valley.
They dont know that theres an entire facility underground.
After these news in the TV you decide to go on a misson to uncover the truth of the weather anomalies.

This is Operation: DIME
